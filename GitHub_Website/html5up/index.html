PATHWAY to FULL STACK DEVELOPER
My goal is to become an effective Full Stack Developer. I must develop my skill sets in the 3 categories of, front-end, back-end, and sales conversion. For me, there is more than just building pretty websites and connecting them to a sophisticated backend. With my background I understand that more often than not the true needs of the sales force and sales support staff get lost in translation. I understand these needs. The web apps and programs I build keep in mind the problems, pains, and fears of a salesforce when they need to adopt a new process.
